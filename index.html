Junkie Alchemist

Published

Goal: Create a game within a strict time limit for the "You shouldn't mis those" jam hosted by Capybara Studios

The jam was part of the Unity Game Developer Bootcamp by Generation Colombia. In this game, you are an alchemist with an addiction. You need to fulfill clients' orders by mixing the necessary ingredients for potions.

However, the withdrawal symptoms are too severe to ignore, so you must constantly drug yourself while working to avoid a crisis. But, you shouldn't mix the drugs, since you could become intoxicated!

Role: Game mechanic programmer

Tools and skills: Unity v 6.3 | C#
