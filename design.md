## Objective
+ Create a portfolio that presents my games as memorable experiences while reflecting my identity as a Game Developer and Game Designer.
+ The portfolio should feel memorable, elegant and professional without losing its personality.
+ The portfolio should encourage visitors to play my projects, explore my creative journey and remember the person behind those experiences.
+ Remember: The main objective is to introduce myself to the professional world of the video game industry

## Public objective
Primary audience
    * Recruiters
Secondary audience
    * Game developers
    * Indie studios

## Color Palette
- Background: #252525
- Font: #F1EBD8
- Main accent: #FF9A3D
- Secondary accent: #58D6E6
- Details: #A8E063

Rules for the colors:
+ Avoid using all accent colors together.
+ Main is for interaction
+ Secondary is for technical information
+ Details color is for development journey

## Typography

- Text: Atkinson Hyperlegible

## Visual style
- Elegant
- Creative
- Joyful
- Dramatic
- Should feel like entering the backstage of a theatre
- Minimalist interface
- Subtle stage inspiration

## Design principles
- Tell a story
- Content first
- Personality over decoratio
- Each section has a purpose
- Simplicity over complexity
- Showcase growth, not perfection -> I' m still learning and that's OK

## Experience Goals
Visitors should think while exploring and after leaving the website:
+ I want to play her games
+ She really loves making games
+ She keeps improving
+ She has personality
+ I want to see her next step

## Design philosophy
The portfolio exists as an invitation to test my games, a showcase for them. Every visual decision should support that goal. The design should feel elegant, warm, and memorable, while letting the projects remain the center of attention. The theatrical inspiration should create atmosphere, not distract from the content.
